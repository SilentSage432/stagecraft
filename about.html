About the Sage

Wandering artisan. Keeper of arcane tradition.

The Sage is the founder of Sage Craft Alchemy, a wandering artisan who weaves the ancient arts into every creation, preserving the arcane traditions for those who seek the extraordinary.

The Story So Far

From the mist-shrouded forests of Eldermoor to the sunlit peaks of the Crystal Spires, the Sage has journeyed far and wide. Guided by whispers of forgotten lore and the flicker of enchanted flames, the Sage's path is one of discovery and reverence for the mysteries that bind the natural and the mystical realms.
